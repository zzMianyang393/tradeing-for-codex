# Daily BIAS Range Reversion: Research Card

- signal: completed UTC daily close is at least 10% below EMA20;
- direction: long at the following open plus a 4h availability delay;
- regime: completed 4h `震荡` label only;
- exit: first close at or above EMA20, or 7 days; stop 2 ATR(14);
- cost: 0.16% round trip; one active observation per symbol; no parameter sweep.

Formation is 2024, OOS is 2025-01-01 to 2025-07-10. Both require >=15 compatible events and positive net mean. This is research-only and never paper eligible.
