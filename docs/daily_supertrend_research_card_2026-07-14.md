# Daily SuperTrend: Research Card

- indicator: SuperTrend using ATR(10) and multiplier 3;
- trigger: completed daily SuperTrend direction flips positive for long or negative for short;
- regime: long only in completed 4h `趋势上行`, short only in `趋势下行`;
- entry: next daily open plus 4h availability delay;
- exit: opposite SuperTrend flip or 10 days; hard stop 2 ATR(14);
- cost: 0.16% round trip; no optimization.

Formation 2024; OOS 2025-01-01 to 2025-07-10. Each needs >=15 events, positive net mean, and <=25% positive-return monthly concentration. Research-only.
